Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Data Collection

At Therapy4U.net, we collect basic account information including your email address and encrypted password to provide secure access to your consultation history. We also temporarily process the text of your messages to facilitate the AI's response generation.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to provide the AI psychoanalysis service. We do not sell your personal information or chat logs to third-party advertisers. BESMIT LIMITED uses anonymized data to improve the quality of our AI models and user experience.

3. Data Retention

Your chat logs are stored securely so you can reference your session history at any time. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

4. Information Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to protect your data during transmission. While no system is 100% secure, we prioritize the confidentiality of your private reflections and consultation history.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use intrusive cross-site tracking or third-party marketing cookies.

6. Secure Payments

Payments for our support plans are handled entirely by Stripe. BESMIT LIMITED does not store or have access to your full credit card details or sensitive billing information on our own servers.

7. International Operations

Therapy4U.net is operated by BESMIT LIMITED in Hong Kong. By using our service, you acknowledge that your information may be processed on servers located outside your home country to ensure service availability.

8. User Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our data privacy officer via the dashboard.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our service or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
